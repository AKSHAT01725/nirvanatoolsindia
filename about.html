Who We Are

Forged From
Gujarat's Heart

Nirvana Tools India Pvt. Ltd. is a premier manufacturer of forged agricultural and industrial hand tools, proudly based at Salal on the Ahmedabad–Himatnagar National Highway (N.H-48) in Sabarkantha, Gujarat, India.

We specialize in the manufacture of a comprehensive range of hoes, spades, shovels, and scrapers using high-carbon steel, combining traditional drop-forging expertise with modern quality standards to deliver tools that perform in demanding field conditions.

Our state-of-the-art plant is equipped to handle bulk production for domestic and international markets. We serve farmers, contractors, infrastructure developers, and government bodies — anyone who needs reliable, long-lasting hand tools.

Why Choose NTI

The NTI Advantage

What sets Nirvana Tools apart from the competition — built on quality, trust, and craftsmanship.

Premium Steel Grade
We use only high-carbon steel sourced from trusted suppliers, ensuring blades that resist deformation and hold a sharp edge under heavy use.
Superior Hardness
Each blade is quench-hardened after forging, achieving high Rockwell hardness for maximum durability in tough agricultural and construction conditions.
Wide Product Range
50+ variants across hoes, spades, and shovels — available in multiple weights, eye-types, and blade profiles to match every application.
Strict Quality Control
Every piece passes dimensional inspection and surface-quality checks before leaving our facility, ensuring consistency across bulk orders.
Competitive Pricing
Direct-from-manufacturer pricing with bulk discounts and flexible payment terms for distributors, retailers, and exporters.
Strategic Location
On N.H-48 Ahmedabad–Himatnagar Highway for fast, efficient dispatch to all parts of India and export ports.
