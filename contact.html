Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What is the minimum order quantity?
We cater to bulk buyers and distributors. Minimum order quantities vary by product. Contact us directly for MOQ and slab pricing details specific to the products you require.
Do you offer OEM / custom branding?
Yes. We offer OEM branding, custom stampings, and private-label packaging for large-volume buyers. We can produce tools to your exact specifications including custom weights, dimensions, and eye types.
Do you export outside India?
Absolutely. We have an active IEC code and export our products internationally. Our packaging complies with export requirements. Please mention your destination country when enquiring for documentation support.
Can I request product samples?
Yes, we can arrange product samples for serious buyers. Sample costs and courier charges may apply. Mention the specific products and sizes you'd like to evaluate in your enquiry.
What payment terms do you offer?
We offer flexible payment terms for established buyers. Bank transfer, RTGS/NEFT, and LC for international orders are accepted. Specific terms are discussed during order confirmation.
How long does delivery take?
Lead times depend on order volume and product availability. Standard stock items can be dispatched quickly from our N.H-48 warehouse. Custom or large volume orders may require 15–30 days production time.
